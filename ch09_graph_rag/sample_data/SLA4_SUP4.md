# Legacy Compute Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Supplier**: SUP4  
**Customer**: Industrial Services GmbH  
**Service**: Compute Classic v1  
**Version**: 2023-01  
**Effective Date**: N/A  
**Governing Law**: France

## 1. Service Description

The Compute Classic v1 platform runs workloads on legacy hardware generations. It is suitable for compatibility or non critical tasks with variable performance expectations.

## 2. Availability and Uptime Commitment

The supplier guarantees **99.5 percent monthly uptime**. Legacy hardware may introduce higher frequency of minor outages.

## 3. Response Times and Incident Handling

- SEV1 outages → **30 minute** response
- Other issues → 2–12 hours depending on scope

## 4. Maintenance Windows

Biweekly maintenance cycles may require VM restarts. Notices are provided **48 hours in advance** when possible.

## 5. Service Credits

- Below 99.5 percent → **10 percent credit**
- Below 98.5 percent → **20 percent credit**

## 6. Customer Responsibilities

Customers must secure and maintain their OS, handle backups and prepare for higher variability due to legacy hardware.

## 7. Data Protection and Privacy

French and EU data protection law applies. Some older logging systems may provide reduced detail.

## 8. Security and Compliance

Due to hardware age, some modern security controls may be unavailable. Encryption in transit is supported; disk encryption may not be.

## 9. Limitations of Liability

Liability is capped at fees paid during the previous 12 months. Customer errors are excluded.

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days notice. Customers must delete all workloads before termination.

## 11. Exclusions

Unsupported features, hardware end of life events, customer misconfiguration and planned maintenance.

## 12. Region Specific Terms

French regulations may require additional reporting obligations. Legacy systems may not support region migration.

## 13. Definitions

- **Legacy Instance**: Workload hosted on aged hardware
- **Downtime**: Service failure interval
- **Service Credit**: Credit applied for SLA violations
